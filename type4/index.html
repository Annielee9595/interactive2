SILHOUETTE


My typeface is called Silhouette, which was inspired by combining lowercase and uppercase. The reason why I combine the lowercases and uppercases together is because this typeface is in solid shape. Usually, we see the lowercases and uppercases separate, and I think it is more interesting to see the combination of lowercases and uppercases. When you click on the uppercase, it displays the lowercase and geometric shapes in each letter. Because of the geometric shapes, I want people to interact with the shapes when they click on the letters and see the combination of uppercase and lowercases since they are already geometric. I also want to illustrate the interaction of color so I make the background color changes while you are moving the mouse.
